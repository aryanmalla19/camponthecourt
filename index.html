Program Details

We will conduct this CAMP ON THE COURT program by keeping the rules under vision. We will motivate and design various session to our players. Here are the list of basketball program and it's session.

WARM UP SESSION

Warm-up and stretching, the strength coaches emphasize, help cut down on injuries. And a hamstring stretch can even make you a better player

DRILLS SESSION

The fundamentals, or the basic skills of basketball, commonly included these five: dribbling (ball handling), shooting, passing, jumping (rebounding), and defense.Our camp also have basic basketball equipment.

COOLDOWN SESSION

The cooldown acts as a reset for the next practice or game. It also reduces the chances of causing the player damage.

PRACTICE AND DRILLS CHAPTER
  1. Fundamentals and Drills
    The three main skills in basketball are dribbling, passing, and shooting. Of course along with these three skills, many other variables come into play when one is trying to develop their overall game
  2. SKILLS CONVERSION
    Some of the most important skills that are part of the game of basketball include shooting, dribbling, defense, rebounding, and passing.
  3. SHOOTING PROGRESIION
    Shooting is the most important skill in basketball. Development of an accurate shot forces your defender to play you tight and become vulnerable to a fake, allowing you to pass and drive as well as shoot.
  4. Defensive System Development
    A basketball defense refers to the defensive strategies, alignments, and positioning that a team uses to prevent the opposing offense from scoring baskets.This is the most important session
  5. Knowlegde of Game and Match Sense
    Every player should have sense of game. Game sense is the ability to pick up on small cues during play that will allow players to anticipate what's going to happen next with a higher probability.
  6. Compeitive Drills (Full court and Half Court)
    After the above session our players will start to do compeitive drills. Actual games provide low repetition and high motivation, whereas drills offer high repetition and low motivation.
  7. Situational Drills, Rules Orirentation of Games
    As we are heading to our ending session. Camp on the court helps the players to understand the game even better. So we make our players do the situational drills and also teach them rues of basketball.
  8. Basketball Film Study
    As this is our last session, we help the players to understand the game of basketball by summarising the individual game and telling the individual one's what needs to be done.
